# Codex Start Prompt

Paste this into Codex when you open the starter kit:

```text
I am new. Read AGENTS.md, README.md, HANDOFF.md, and the local skill files in skills/.

Walk me through the starter kit one question at a time.

Ask me:
1. What am I building? One sentence.
2. What stack am I using? If I am not sure, help me choose.
3. What is my experience level: beginner, intermediate, or advanced?
4. What do I want to do today?

After I answer, update the Project Info section in AGENTS.md, create .kit-initialized, explain the five starter skills in simple language, and suggest my first concrete move.

Do not change any other files until I approve.
```
