# Devils Advocate

Use this skill when you need pushback before shipping or committing to a plan.

## Goal

Catch weak assumptions, missing proof, unclear scope, and risky decisions.

## Review Questions

1. What assumption could be wrong?
2. What would fail for the user?
3. What is over-scoped?
4. What is under-tested?
5. What simpler version would still work?

## Output

Lead with findings:

1. Highest-risk issue.
2. Why it matters.
3. What to change.
4. What can wait.

If there are no major issues, say that clearly and list the remaining risk.
