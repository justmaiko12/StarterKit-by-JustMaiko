# UI/UX Pro Max

Use this skill when reviewing a screen, page, or app flow.

## Goal

Make the product easier to understand, easier to use, and more credible.

## Review Checklist

1. What is the user trying to do?
2. Is the main action obvious in 3 seconds?
3. Is anything visually fighting for attention?
4. Is the layout too sparse, too crowded, or poorly grouped?
5. Are labels, buttons, and empty states clear?
6. Does the design fit the product category?
7. What is the smallest change that improves the screen?

## Output

Give feedback in this order:

1. Biggest issue.
2. Why it matters.
3. Specific fix.
4. Optional next improvement.

Keep the review practical. Do not redesign the whole product unless asked.
